This cluster examines how media technologies shape our perception of reality. Drawing from critical theory, media studies, and postmodern philosophy, we explore how reproduction, spectacle, and simulation create the world we experience.

The key insight is that in a media-saturated world, the distinction between reality and representation becomes increasingly difficult to maintain.
