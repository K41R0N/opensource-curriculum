This cluster challenges the notion that tools are neutral. Drawing from science and technology studies, material culture studies, and philosophy of technology, we examine how the design and use of devices embeds values and shapes possibilities.

The key insight is that devices are never merely instrumental—they always carry intentions and consequences beyond their stated purposes.
